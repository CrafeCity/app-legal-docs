Privacy Policy
Last Updated: 17-3-2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ewmaly Fit ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information. We prioritize your privacy by keeping your health and fitness data entirely on your device.

1. Health and Fitness Data (Apple HealthKit & Google Health Connect)
To provide accurate metabolic calculations and automated tracking, Ewmaly Fit requests read access to specific health metrics (such as steps, weight, nutrition, and sleep) via Apple HealthKit (iOS) and Google Health Connect (Android).

Local Processing Only: All data read from Apple Health or Google Health Connect is processed and stored locally on your device.

No Cloud Storage: We do not transmit, sync, or store your health data on external servers.

Strict Prohibitions: Your health data is never used for marketing, advertising, or sold to third parties under any circumstances.

2. Local Data Storage
All user-entered data, including your profile settings, macro targets, weight logs, and daily check-ins, is stored locally on your device using local storage mechanisms. If you uninstall the app or clear the app's data, this information is permanently deleted. We do not maintain a remote database of your personal information.

3. Third-Party Services
While your health data remains strictly local, we use the following third-party services to operate the app's infrastructure and monetization:

RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage Pro subscriptions. RevenueCat processes your purchase history and subscription status. It does not have access to your health or physical data.

Google AdMob (Free Tier Only): For users on the free tier, we display banner and app-open advertisements. We have explicitly configured AdMob to serve Non-Personalized Ads (NPA) only. This means ads are not targeted based on your past behavior or personal data, though AdMob may still use contextual information and device identifiers for frequency capping and fraud prevention. Pro users do not load or see ads.

4. Data Retention and Deletion
Because your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. You can delete all your data at any time by:

Using the "Clear Data" option in the app's Settings page.

Uninstalling the app from your device.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
